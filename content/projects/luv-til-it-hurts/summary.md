From 2018-2020, Luv ’til it Hurts engaged poz artists and allies from around the world to understand their local HIV-related stigmas and challenges. Intended as its R&D phase, the project developed a malleable device for deployment to the global AIDS front called Exquisite Corpse, an evolving group show, interactive game and soapbox that can be homemade and occur wherever needed.

The LUV process and resulting Exquisite Corpse are the fruit of many artists and organizers working together, including Jakub Szczęsny, Brad Walrond, Sebastien Sanz de Santamaria, Adham Bakry, Paula Nishijima, ANKH Association, Daniel Santiago Salguero and Love Positive Women.
